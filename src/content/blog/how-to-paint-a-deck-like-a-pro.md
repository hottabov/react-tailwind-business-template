A tired grey deck can normally be brought back to life with the right preparation and products.

## Step 1: Inspect the Timber

Look for soft, spongey boards or black stains around screws. Replace any rotten sections before you think about coating.

## Step 2: Clean Thoroughly

Use a dedicated deck cleaner, scrub it in and rinse with a pressure washer. Let the deck dry completely.

## Step 3: Sand the Surface

Sand along the grain to remove raised fibres and old coating. You want a smooth but slightly open surface.

## Step 4: Choose Oil, Stain or Paint

- Clear oil keeps the natural look but needs more frequent maintenance  
- Semi‑transparent stain adds colour while showing grain  
- Solid deck paint hides imperfections and gives the longest protection

Apply two thin coats and avoid painting in direct hot sun.
