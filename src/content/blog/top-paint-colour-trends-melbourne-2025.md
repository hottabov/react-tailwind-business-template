Trends come and go, but they do influence what buyers expect to see in a modern Melbourne home. Used carefully, they make your home feel current without dating quickly.

## 1. Warm Neutrals

Cool greys are on the way out. Warm beiges and “greige” tones feel softer and more inviting in Melbourne's climate.

## 2. Dusty Greens

Soft sage and eucalyptus greens are everywhere — bedrooms, bathrooms and living rooms. They pair beautifully with timber and linen.

## 3. Earthy Accent Colours

Terracotta, clay and rust tones are ideal for feature walls and entry niches. They add depth without feeling loud.

## 4. Off‑White Everything

Instead of pure white, designers are favouring slightly warm off‑whites. They hide imperfections better and feel less clinical.
