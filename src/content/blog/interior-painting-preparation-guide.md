Ask any professional painter and you'll hear the same thing: preparation is everything. Rushing prep is how you end up with peeling, bubbling or patchy paint within two years.

## Tools You Will Need

- Flexible filler knife and pre‑mix filler  
- Sanding blocks (80, 120 and 220 grit)  
- Painter's tape  
- Drop sheets  
- Primer

## Step 1: Clear and Protect

Move furniture away from walls, cover everything with drop sheets and remove switch plates and curtain rods.

## Step 2: Clean the Surfaces

Paint does not stick to grease and dust. Wash the walls with sugar soap, then let them dry completely.

## Step 3: Repair Defects

Fill holes and cracks, let the filler dry and sand smooth. For hairline cracks use flexible acrylic filler.

## Step 4: Sand and Prime

Lightly sand the whole wall, vacuum the dust and wipe with a damp cloth. Finish with a quality primer so the top coats can perform properly.
