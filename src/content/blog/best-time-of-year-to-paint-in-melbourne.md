Melbourne's four seasons in one day can make planning a painting project tricky. Timing the job for the right part of the year helps paint cure properly and reduces delays.

## Exterior Painting

Mild, dry weather is ideal. Late spring and early autumn usually offer the best combination of temperature and humidity.

Avoid very hot days, strong winds and weeks with heavy rain forecast.

## Interior Painting

Interior work is more flexible, but humidity and temperature still affect drying times. In winter, good ventilation and gentle heating make a big difference.

## Booking Ahead

The best painters book out weeks or months in advance for popular seasons, so contact them early if you have a deadline.
