The roof is the most exposed surface of your home, yet it is often the last thing people think about. Roof painting is not just cosmetic; done properly, it is a protective treatment.

## What Roof Painting Can Do

- Seal micro‑cracks in concrete tiles  
- Slow down UV damage  
- Reduce moss and lichen growth  
- Improve kerb appeal dramatically

## When It Makes Sense

Roof painting is a good idea when the roof is structurally sound but faded, chalky or patchy. It is not a replacement for major structural repairs.

## Typical Costs in Melbourne

Most single‑storey homes fall somewhere between a few thousand dollars for full cleaning, repairs and membrane coating. That is usually far less than a full re‑roof.
