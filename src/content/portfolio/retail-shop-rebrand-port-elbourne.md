Retail doesn’t stop.

So we painted while the city slept.

## Project

Boutique store in **Port Melbourne (~90 m² retail space)** undergoing rebrand.

Constraint:  
👉 **No downtime**

## Plan

- Work overnight (8pm – 6am)  
- Finish in 2 nights  

## Challenges

- Strong existing colours (deep red walls)  
- Needed full coverage in limited time  
- Drying time between coats  

## Execution

### Night 1
- Full prep + first coat  
- High-opacity primer  

### Night 2
- 2 finish coats  
- Feature wall in brand colour  

## Materials

- Low-VOC paints (important for morning opening)  
- Fast-drying systems  

## Budget

- ~$2,700 AUD

## Incident

At 3am — fire alarm triggered.

Not by us.

Still cost us 40 minutes.

No excuses — job finished anyway.

## Result

Store opened next morning.

Customers thought it was a new business.

Owner said:
> “This feels like a different brand now.”
