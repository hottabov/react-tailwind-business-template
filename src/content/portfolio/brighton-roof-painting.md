Roofs don’t fail suddenly. They decay quietly.

This one in **Brighton (~180 m² roof area)** had been ignored for 15+ years.

Faded colour, heavy lichen growth, and tiles absorbing water like a sponge.

## What We Found

- Moss and lichen covering ~40%  
- Surface erosion on tile edges  
- Previous patch repairs with mismatched colours  

## Process

Total time: **3 days**

### Day 1 — Cleaning
- High-pressure wash (3,000 PSI)
- Chemical treatment to kill organic growth  

### Day 2 — Repairs & Primer
- Replaced 17 cracked tiles  
- Sealed porous surfaces  
- Applied roof membrane primer  

### Day 3 — Coating
- 2 coats Dulux Acratex Roof Membrane  
- Satin charcoal finish  

## Budget

- Approx: **$4,800 AUD**

## Unexpected Moment

Halfway through cleaning, the roof turned patchy — client panicked.

Thought we ruined it.

Reality: that’s what **real condition** looks like when dirt is gone.

We explained. They calmed down.

## Result

- Even, consistent finish  
- Improved water resistance  
- House visually dropped 10–15 years in age  

Client message next day:
> “Didn’t realise the roof could change the whole house this much.”
