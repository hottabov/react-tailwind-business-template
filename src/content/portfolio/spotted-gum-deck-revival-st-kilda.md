Years of sun and sea air had turned this beautiful spotted gum deck a flat grey. The surface felt rough and the old coating was patchy.

## What We Did

- Stripped the failing coating with a deck remover  
- Thoroughly washed and neutralised the timber  
- Sanded the boards to remove raised fibres  
- Applied two coats of premium decking oil

## The Difference

The grain and warmth of the timber are now the hero of the outdoor space. The surface feels smooth underfoot and water beads on the surface instead of soaking in.
