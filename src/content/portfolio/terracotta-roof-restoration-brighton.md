This Brighton home had a beautiful terracotta roof that was hidden under moss, lichen and years of grime.

## Restoration Process

- Treated the roof with an anti-fungal wash  
- Gently pressure cleaned tiles to avoid damage  
- Replaced cracked and slipped tiles  
- Re-pointed ridge caps where necessary  
- Applied a clear protective sealer

## Outcome

Instead of a full re-roof, the owners achieved a “like new” appearance at a fraction of the cost. The cleaned terracotta also helps shed water more efficiently and reduces future moss growth.
