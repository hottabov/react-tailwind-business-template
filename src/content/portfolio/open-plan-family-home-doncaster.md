The previous owners loved feature colours; every room was different. Our clients wanted something calmer and more cohesive.

## Scope

- Full interior repaint, including ceilings and trims  
- Neutral, family-friendly palette with low-VOC paints  
- Focus on durability for kids' bedrooms and hallways

## Result

The new scheme ties the open-plan living, dining and kitchen areas together. Artwork and furniture now stand out against the softer background.
