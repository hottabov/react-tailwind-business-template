This Richmond apartment had been a rental for years. The walls were marked, the ceilings were dull and the colour scheme felt flat.

## Scope of Work

- Full repaint of living, kitchen, bedrooms and hallway  
- Nicotine and cooking stain blocking to ceilings  
- New feature wall in the living area to anchor the sofa  
- Fresh white doors, frames and skirting boards

## Result

By keeping the palette light and consistent, we made the small apartment feel larger and more open. The agent reported strong buyer feedback and multiple offers after the first inspection weekend.
