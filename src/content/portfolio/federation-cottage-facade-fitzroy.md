This classic Federation cottage had beautiful fretwork and details that were lost under layers of old, peeling paint.

## Careful Preparation

- Hand-scraped failing paint from decorative timber  
- Spot-primed bare sections with an oil-based primer  
- Repaired minor timber rot in trims  
- Used small brushes to preserve sharp lines on details

## Colour Story

We selected a muted heritage colour scheme with a deep front door colour to add character without overpowering the facade.

The result is a home that looks freshly restored rather than freshly renovated.
