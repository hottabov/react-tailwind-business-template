The existing roof and gutter colours clashed with the freshly painted render. Rather than repaint the walls again, the owners chose to update the metalwork.

## Process

- Washdown of roof sheets and gutters  
- Spot-primed any rusted areas  
- Applied a heat-reflective roof coating  
- Repainted gutters, fascias and downpipes to match

## Visual Impact

Changing the roof and gutter colour simplified the facade and made the home look more expensive, without touching the walls.
