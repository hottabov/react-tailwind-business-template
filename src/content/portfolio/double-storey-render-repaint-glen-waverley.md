The original cream render on this Glen Waverley home had become patchy and stained. The owners wanted a cleaner, more contemporary street presence.

## Our Approach

- Pressure washed the entire house to remove dirt and mould  
- Repaired hairline cracks in the render  
- Sealed problem areas with an elastomeric primer  
- Applied two coats of a high-build exterior acrylic

## Colour Scheme

We used a mid-grey for the main render and a darker charcoal for architectural features, keeping trims a fresh white.

The combination highlights the home's lines and makes it look like a brand new build.
