Our client wanted their Southbank office to better reflect their brand — modern, clean and professional — without losing too many trading days.

## Scheduling & Execution

We completed the bulk of the work over a single weekend:

- Friday evening: Preparation and priming  
- Saturday: Walls, ceilings and trims  
- Sunday: Second coats and touch-ups

## Design Choices

We used a neutral off-white for the main walls, with a deep blue feature wall behind the reception desk and subtle grey in meeting rooms to reduce glare on screens.

Staff returned on Monday to what felt like a brand new workspace.
