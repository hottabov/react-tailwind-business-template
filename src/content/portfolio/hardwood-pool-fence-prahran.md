The hardwood fence around this Prahran pool had gone pale and dry, blending into the background instead of framing the space.

## Preparation & Products

- Light sand to remove the grey oxidised surface  
- Washdown to remove dust and contaminants  
- Two coats of a UV-stable exterior oil in a warm walnut tone

## Final Look

The deeper colour now contrasts beautifully with the pale stone and blue pool water. The timber is protected from further UV damage and will be easy to maintain with future re-coats.
