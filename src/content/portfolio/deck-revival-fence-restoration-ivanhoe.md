Timber doesn’t forgive neglect.

This deck in **Ivanhoe (~45 m²)** had been left untreated for years.

Grey, dry, and starting to crack.

## What We Found

- UV damage across entire surface  
- Splitting boards in high-traffic areas  
- Old stain uneven and flaking  

## Process

Total: **2 days**

### Day 1
- Sanding entire deck  
- Replacing 3 damaged boards  
- Deep clean  

### Day 2
- 2 coats of oil-based decking stain  
- Fence refresh to match tone  

## Materials

- Intergrain UltraDeck (natural finish)

## Budget

- ~$1,900 AUD

## The Moment

After first coat — colour came back instantly.

Client just stood there and said:
> “I forgot it looked like this.”

## Result

- Rich natural tone restored  
- Water resistance improved  
- Outdoor area usable again  

Not dramatic.

But quietly satisfying.
