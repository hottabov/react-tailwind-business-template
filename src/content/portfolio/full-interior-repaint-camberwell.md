This wasn’t about taste. This was about money.

Property in **Camberwell (~140 m² interior)** going to market in 2 weeks.

Agent said:
> “Neutral sells.”

The existing colours said the opposite.

## Before

- Dark feature walls in every room  
- Uneven patch repairs  
- Scuffed trims and doors  

## Strategy

We didn’t just repaint — we **designed for resale**.

- Warm neutral walls (Lexicon Quarter tone)  
- Crisp white trims  
- Ceiling refresh for light reflection  

## Timeline

- Total: **4 days**
- Team: 3 painters

## Materials

- Dulux Wash&Wear (walls)
- Dulux Aquanamel (doors & trims)

## Cost

- ~$3,200 AUD

## The Turning Point

Day 3 — everything looked worse.

Patchy, uneven, unfinished.

Client nervous.

We told them: **trust the process**.

## Result

Final day — completely different space.

- Brighter  
- Bigger feeling  
- Cleaner lines  

House sold in **9 days**.

Above expected price.

Paint didn’t sell the house — but it removed every reason not to buy it.
