This hospitality space started as a bare concrete and plaster shell. Our job was to bring the designer's concept to life with paint.

## Key Elements

- Deep charcoal ceiling to hide services and create intimacy  
- Warm neutral walls to complement brick and timber  
- Hand-painted feature wall with a soft colour wash effect  
- Durable, washable finishes behind the bar and in high-traffic areas

## Outcome

The finished space feels cosy and inviting during evening service, while still bright enough for a brunch crowd.
