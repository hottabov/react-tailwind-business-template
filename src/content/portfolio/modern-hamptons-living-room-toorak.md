This Toorak living room had great bones — high ceilings, timber floors and natural light — but the brown walls and yellowed ceilings dragged everything down.

## What We Did

- Repaired hairline cracks and old picture hook holes  
- Applied a stain-blocking ceiling primer  
- Painted ceilings and cornices in a crisp warm white  
- Walls finished in a soft Hamptons-inspired greige  
- High-wear trims in a tough semi-gloss enamel

## Before vs After

Before, the room felt small and heavy. After repainting, the space feels taller, brighter and much more in line with the client's Hamptons furniture and styling.

The owners were so happy with the result that we have since repainted their master bedroom and hallway using the same palette.
