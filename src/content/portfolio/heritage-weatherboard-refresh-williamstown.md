This heritage weatherboard cottage had peeling paint, exposed timber and serious UV damage on the western side.

## Preparation

- Full washdown to remove chalky old paint  
- Scraping, sanding and spot-priming bare timber  
- Gap filling and minor carpentry repairs  
- Rust treatment on metal fixtures

## Painting & Colours

We used a soft coastal grey for the boards, crisp white for trims and a deep navy on the front door for contrast. All coatings were premium exterior paints designed for coastal conditions.

## The Transformation

The home now feels fresh and cared for, and the owners have peace of mind knowing their timber is protected for years to come.
